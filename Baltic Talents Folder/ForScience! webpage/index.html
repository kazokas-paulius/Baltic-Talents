Q&A

The very very VERY beginning ...

The "For Sciene!" project as idea appeared a few days before disclosure of our University's Diplomas topic day. There was multiple MMORGP ideas, so I called my friend with this idea to create a horror game and transfer it into virtual reality. Through all study years both of us never done such thing with Unity. It was a challenge. After a quick call on a phone and other couple hour call on Skype we accepted this idea.

What is "For Science!"?

For Science! is virtual reality horror, puzzle, exploration game made with Unity3D and coded in C#.

What gear is required to play game?

Smartphone, VR headset (tested and developed using Google Cardboard v1.0) and Bluetooth Controller.

What platforms game supports?

Game is currently built for Android 7.0+ smartphones only.

What else I should know before playing game?

We recommend using smartphones that has 5" size screen, it's ideal size to play the game because you can't feel the motion sickness in virtual world and it's the best way to enjoy the game.

Warning: spending long hours playing the game may cause head dizzing, eye watering, eye pain and nausea. Play responsibly!
